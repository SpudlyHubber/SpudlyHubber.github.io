Welcome To Wordle Extreme!

This is Wordle Extreme - a harder version of Wordle where you're told how many letters are right in the right places and right in the wrong places - just not which ones! You're going to have to make assumptions that may or may not be true. You've got an unlimited number of guesses - but potential points decrease with every guess, so try and get the answer in as little guesses as possible. Points contribute to your total score and determine your rank. Happy wordling!
